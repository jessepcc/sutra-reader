# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
verbatim.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainers through the channel described in
[SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md). All complaints will be reviewed promptly and
fairly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of
acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in
response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or
harmful.
